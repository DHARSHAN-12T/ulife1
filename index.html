Cancer Care Unit

We offer comprehensive cancer treatment plans tailored to individual needs, including chemotherapy and support services. Our Cancer Care Unit is designed to provide a comforting and supportive environment for patients during their treatment journey.

Our specialized team guides patients through their journey with empathy and expert care. From diagnosis to recovery, we focus on providing holistic support that addresses both the physical and emotional aspects of cancer treatment.



  • Personalized treatment plans based on individual needs
  • Access to the latest chemotherapy and radiation therapies
  • Support groups and counseling for patients and families
  • Nutrition and wellness programs to enhance recovery
  • Comprehensive follow-up care to monitor progress
